Do I have to steer the sled myself?

All packages except for the minimalist packages include a chauffeur. So you'll only have to steer if you take the minimalist package. But even then you can add an optional chauffeur.

How do I pay for the packages?

We only accept the American Express Centurion Card or BitCoin. We charge 40% of the final price up front the other 60% needs to be payed within 30 days after delivery. Refunds are granted on a case by case basis and only cover up to 60% of the total price.

Are the designs of the packages final?

The design of the buildings are final but you can customise the interiors. As for the other packages, we appreciate your input and will try to implement anything extra you might want.
